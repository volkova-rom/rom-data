Best Blacksmith in the Land Heroic Passive
Base Effect: Each time you do the 'swing hammer' animation through certain class-specific skills, there is a chance to upgrade the refine level of one of your non-headgear equipment slot at random for 30s.
The upgraded refine levels can go above +15, and cannot be dispelled but are lost on death
The chance to upgrade increases for every heroic passive level, at 40/50/60/70/80/90/100%
The skills that trigger the 'swing hammer' animation are Destructive Hammer, Focus Mode, and Lucky Refine
Additional Effect on Lv4+: When the refine levels of your offhand, armor, garment, and shoes are at least +12, when receiving physical or magical damage, the attacker's Ignore PDEF and Ignore MDEF are set to 0.
Additional Effect on Lv7: When the refine levels of your main weapon and both of your accessories are at least +17, your refine PATK gets boosted by your PATK% stat.
